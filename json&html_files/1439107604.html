    Description

    Simple app for tracking breast milk storage. Easily inventory your pumped breastmilk in your fridge, freezer or deep freeze. No more guessing about what is currently in your stash!


    Features:

    -Track milk in up to 10 locations

    -Easily see total milk in each storage location

    -Calculates how long your milk will last based on your baby's consumption

    -Pumping calculator that allows you to adjust values to see when you can stop pumping

    -Get notified when milk is expiring

    -Track in mL or oz

    -Add caregivers who can view and edit your stash

    -Stores a backup of your milk stash remotely


    This app is built and maintained by one mama alone, so if you come across bugs or features you'd like to see, please email me directly through the app. I always love to help out!
