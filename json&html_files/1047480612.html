    CKD - Go! 17+

    All CKD related content.

    Digital Noir Pty Ltd

    Designed for iPhone

      • Free

    iPhone Screenshots

    Description

    This app is a clinical guide based upon the latest evidence available at the time of publication of the CKD Management in Primary Care 5th edition, 2024, Kidney Health Australia.


    The information is designed to provide health-care professionals with clinical actions, assist decision-making and be available at the click of a button on your personal device.


    The CKD-Go! app includes a CKD staging calculator using eGFR and urine albumin:creatinine ratio data to identify clinical action plans relevant for the stage of CKD. Staging criteria is adapted from CKD Management in Primary Care 5th edition, all clinical action should consider an individual's circumstances.
