    Description

    The ISICEM 2024 app provides practical information for all participants at the 43th International Symposium on Intensive Care and Emergency Medicine: rapid access to the main program, information about the scientific exhibition and associated meetings, the chance to make your own meeting agenda and maps of the conference center to help you find your way around, and so much more…
