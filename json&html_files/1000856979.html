    Description

    Baylor Scott & White’s MyBSWHealth app gives you the ability to manage all of your healthcare needs in one place.


    Within the app, you can:

    • Find doctors and locations in your network and schedule appointments with ease

    • Securely communicate with your care team

    • View lab results and past visit summaries

    • Review and pay bills

    • Complete a telehealth visit and get a diagnosis and prescription on your smart phone without leaving home

    • View deductible, out of pocket max, and claims information if you are a member of the Scott and White Health Plan

    • Transfer or refill prescriptions at BSW pharmacies

    • Upload health and fitness data, including data from the “Apple Health app”, when enrolled in self-tracking programs

    • Manage your family’s healthcare needs from a single place


    The MyBSWHealth app is just one way that Baylor Scott & White is making healthcare the way it should be.

    Robnation101 , 01/25/2024

    App Needs Some Improvements

    I access this app to view documents, appointments, test results and communicate with drs offices for my 77yo father. I’m able to navigate things myself but it’s not very user friendly for older patients. This should be streamlined in a way that makes it much easier for older patients to navigate and access their information. For example accessing test results takes extra clicks to links within messages to access when they could be simplified by clicking on “test results” and viewing a list of results by date with a comprehensive naming for the result so that I know what type of test it was before opening them. Instead I have to click “test results” and then click a link in a message to view them not knowing what results I’m viewing until they open. Also, I would suggest adding a feature for archiving messages in bulk or at least having an option to slide left or right to mark messages as “read”. There are multiple other improvements like these that could improve the user experience for this app and more importantly make this easier to navigate for elder patients. Overall I’m able to get the information I need for my dad and give it 4 stars. However, I doubt that the elder community currently utilizes this much due to the difficulty they likely have with navigation and overall user experience.
