    Description

    Tried it all, but the pain remains? Let the Curable app show you a (very) different path forward.


    Get started on your Curable journey for free by chatting with our virtual coach, who will design a custom program tailored to your symptoms and preferences. During your journey, you’ll learn about the latest pain science through bite-sized audio lessons, then apply what you’ve learned using 100+ science-backed exercises for relief.


    Your pain experience is unique, and your recovery program should be, too. Depending on your needs, Curable can help you to overcome fear of movement, reduce health anxiety, relax the nervous system, navigate flare-ups, and more.


    Hundreds of thousands of people in pain have used Curable to help them transform their relationship to back pain, migraine, shoulder pain, neck pain, knee pain, arthritis, fibromyalgia, trigeminal neuralgia, post-surgical pain, ME/CFS, sciatica, tinnitus, and more.


    Curable is not a tool to monitor your symptoms - it’s a tool to help you transform them. 69% of Curable users begin to experience physical symptom reduction in the first 30 days of use. We hope that you will be next!

    I love this app! It does what it says!

    This app is so good! If you have any uncontrollable pain, or even a persistent discomfort, this app can help you. There is a wealth of knowledge that helps you understand that your brain is what tells us that we have pain. All pain originates in our brain - this does NOT mean the pain is in our heads, but that our brains generate the amount of pain we feel. We can also change our brain’s response to something that has caused us pain, and therefore reduce the pain. Knowledge is power, and in this case it is a scientifically proven, therapeutic way to reduce pain. I also love the recovery stories and expert interviews. There is guided meditation to help you relax (not my favorite because I don’t like her voice - I use a different exercise app), writing activities, and brain training. The only thing I wish were improved was that there would be more education and brain training into opportunities. For me, these alone helped get rid of my pain. It also reduced or eliminated other uncomfortable sensations I was feeling in my body (e.g., a really weird metallic/sour taste in my mouth I experienced for 9 months with no other medical explanation). I really think it was stress, and this app took it away for me! Please don’t hesitate to buy this app. It is worth it’s weight in gold (and cost of medical and psychotherapy sessions)!

    40 years of pain GONE

    I am 57 years old, and I’ve been suffering from back pain since I was in my teens. For many years as a teenager I saw a physical therapist to help with my back pain. Unfortunately, it only helped a little. I live a very healthy lifestyle, and about 3 to 5 times a year my back will go out, and I will be completely debilitated for several days. Sometimes I am completely unable to even get off the floor or get out of bed as the pain is unbearable.


    The only thing I have found that helps is to continually exercise. Over the years I’ve consulted with many surgeons that said I was not a candidate for surgery. A few years ago I started getting epidural injections that would minimize the frequency of my severe lower back spasms. Of course I don’t like getting the injections and I haven’t had one in over 14 months.


    2 1/2 months ago, I started using the Curable app and I’ve been 90% pain-free. I still have experienced back spasms, but unlike previously when I would be bedridden for a day or two and have to take pain pills and anti inflammatories and miss work for a few days, now my spasms go away within about a minute and so does the pain. I’m blown away by the results and am enjoying the process along the way. I honestly thought that I was going to live with this condition for the rest of my life, but now I know there’s a better way.
