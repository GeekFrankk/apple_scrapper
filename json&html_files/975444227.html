    Description

    MyZio® is the perfect companion app to the Zio® ECG monitor. Use the app to track your monitors shipping status, enter your symptoms, and access helpful information while wearing your Zio ECG monitor.


    Key Features:

    • Signup for a MyZio account anytime

    • Track your monitor’s shipping status

    • Seamlessly register your Zio ECG monitor and start logging symptoms.

    • Enter your symptoms. View and edit your symptoms as needed.

    • Access information about the Zio service. Instructional videos are included to help you throughout your Zio experience.


    Features vary by market and country.

    Update disaster

    Received a new patch and put it on. I know I have to update the serial number in the app. Opened the app and was greeted with the message that the certificate of the server is … (the rest of the test isn’t visible, I assume the missing word is invalid or expired). Call #1: instructed to remove app and install new one. I tried this but was notified that my iOS is too old but that I could run an older version. Which I did and got the same message. Call #2 suggested I use the written log. So I switched to my iPad, downloaded the app and signed in. Message says that my wear period has ended. This is correct; it’s why I need to update the serial number. But I can’t because the only visible controls are the play button to view a video on shipping it back and the home icon, which does nothing. Call #3 informs me to turn up the brightness, which I do. He says I need to access my profile, but of course there is no way to do this. On top of this he asked me for my password! Hard to imagine any reasonable modern system where that would be tolerated. He also said this is a known problem that has been reported by others. I don’t know which would be worse, that he made that up or that it is true and he didn’t start with that information.
