    Description

    WatchPAT™ONE product is a used as a diagnostic aid for the detection of sleep related breathing disorders.

    Caution: Federal law restricts the WatchPAT™ONE device to sale by or on the order of a licensed healthcare practitioner. Please use the WatchPAT™ APP only if you were provided with the WatchPAT™ONE device through a licensed healthcare practitioner. Please seek a doctor’s advice in addition to using this APP and before making any medical decisions.

    A few tips. If you follow the instructions to the letter, you won’t have any issues. Go step by step. If at any point the device isn’t working, contact Lofta. Their customer service is awesome. Be patient with the technology. Make sure it’s connected (green light) and that the watch is not too tight. Attach the sensor on your chest with some medical tape. Don’t put the finger with the sensor over your head or higher than your heart and you won’t have discomfort/throbbing. Be patient. Relax.
