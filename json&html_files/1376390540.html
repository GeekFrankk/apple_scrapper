    Description

    Thanks for checking out this app! My name is Ellis Rochelson — I'm a pediatric cardiologist, electrophysiologist, and novice programmer.


    This is a pocket reference guide for those taking care of children with congenital or acquired heart disease. The goal is to offer quick answers to questions we face in our daily practice.


    I would love to hear any feedback regarding the content or programming of this app — please email me at rochelson.ellis@gmail.com with any suggestions you may have.
