    Description

    Minimize the day-to-day burden on caregivers and administrators with the convenient, user-friendly, WellSky Personal Care mobile app (formerly ClearCare Caregiver Go). Users can easily manage schedules, shifts, tasks, and profiles from the convenience of their mobile device. Designed with caregivers and administrators in mind, the WellSky Personal Care app makes delivering quality care — not the paperwork — the priority.

    Great Company App: Needs a few tweaks

    Overall, reliable and adaptive tube app system of clocking in and out when working with your own clients. What could be improved is the meter location feature, as a caregiver needs to be “In range” of the client’s home to work providing early. I wish there would be a more accurate meter system that operates as soon as you would log-in without lag. More importantly, this app would be more efficient upon an update feature to detail the client’s care assessment and account page categories for when new caregivers get assigned new clients, there would be a more understood process of how this app operates. Again, this app has greater potential and so far I’m enjoying it. Of course, a greater attention to my two suggestions would definitely benefit the user.
