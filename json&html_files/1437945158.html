    Description

    Access Ambra Health's award-winning medical image management suite right from the palm of your hand. Users can effortlessly view imaging from anywhere, scroll through a series, and utilize radiology tooling like zooming, pinching, and rotating all from the palm of their hand. While not intended for diagnostic use, imaging can be securely shared directly from within the app to other physicians, administrators, and patients for enhanced communication and collaboration.

    asrhjmnbvc , 06/25/2024

    Patient’s who can’t use Ambra, here’s how to have a great experience

    As a patient, you can gain access to this program, but you need help from your participating health care program. Basically, you just need to have their records department share an old Ambra image with your email address. Then, sign in to your account by going through your health service (MyChart, for instance) then Test Results, get an automatic sign in link to view your imaging. Once you’re there, select all your imaging, and share them to your email address. Now that you have an Ambra account, and all your records are available through your Ambra account, all of your records will be available through this app. Note: After each new imaging, you’ll need to access Ambra through your health service account, through the Ambra portal, and then it’ll also be available. I’ve had to figure this all out through being diagnosed with multiple sclerosis, getting stable imaging results, and learning as much as I can. MRIs are paramount to the status of multiple sclerosis patients, and this app has earned 5-stars for me!!
