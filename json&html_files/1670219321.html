    Description

    Manage all your health care with one app. Upgraded features make it easier to schedule visits, search health info and connect your entire family’s care.


    • Find doctors, schedule or reschedule appointments

    • Review visit summaries, test results, medications and more

    • Securely communicate with your care team

    • Review and pay bills

    • Complete digital and virtual care visits, all from your phone

    • Manage your family’s health care from one place
