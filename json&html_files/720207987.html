    Description

    Allē is your go-to app for your aesthetic needs, giving you access to curated content, treatment information, exclusive offers, and your Allē Wallet.


    With Allē, you can earn and save on aesthetic products and treatments at your participating provider.


    Allē’s app gives you access to all that Allē offers, including Allē Flash, which enables you to scan for a surprise offer while at your provider’s office.


    Simply log in to your Allē Account within the app and you’re all set. Or, download the app and create a new account in a few simple steps.


    Now, let’s see what’s inside. With the Allē app, you can:


    Scan for Surprise Savings In-Office:

    Earn a surprise offer at your Allē provider’s office. Just scan the Allē Flash QR code when you arrive, and you may receive an additional offer that can be redeemed instantly.


    Browse Offers from the Palm of your Hand:

    Take advantage of exclusive offers for Allē Members that you can redeem right away.


    Read up on the Latest in Aesthetic Treatments:

    Learn about products and treatments that might be right for you. Search for Allē treatments and browse educational articles within the app.


    Access your Wallet in a Few Taps:

    Check your point balance and your membership status, view your transaction history, available offers, and more.


    Purchase and Send Gift Cards to other Allē Members:

    Never worry about losing a gift card again. Allē’s digital gift cards are added directly to your Wallet upon purchase and can be easily gifted to other Allē Members.


    The Allē app makes it easier than ever to manage your aesthetics rewards. It’s all just a tap away. Download today.
