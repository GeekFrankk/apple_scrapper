    Description

    Estimate your body temperature, log and track your data, making it easier to spot potential fever symptoms—all without a thermometer!


    Knowing your temperature is crucial for maintaining well-being and taking the best care of yourself, so stay proactive about your health by monitoring your body temperature with our app. With just a few simple steps on your mobile device, you can track your body temperature trends and take proactive steps towards your health goals.



    But wait – there's more:



    • Find out, track and log your body temperature with ease.


    • Log and easily export your body temperature data and more into PDF to share with your doctor.


    • Easily measure wrist temperature with the Apple Watch app and save your data in our app. (requires Apple Watch 8 or newer)


    • Use your phone’s camera to estimate your heart rate and then easily log your data afterward.


    • Track, log, and export your symptoms and medications effortlessly.


    • Monitor the heat index based on location to safeguard yourself against heat-related conditions that could pose health risks.


    • Add family members and monitor the body temperature of each.


    • Discover how weather conditions can influence your body temperature using our widget.




    Whether you're at home or on the go, managing your health has never been easier.


    The Body Temperature App for Fever is free to download and utilize. However, users can opt for a subscription to access additional features. Your subscription will automatically renew and you’ll be billed based on the plan you choose. You can cancel your subscription at any time in the App Store.


    Disclaimer: For wellness use only. The App is intended to improve your awareness of general wellness. The App can't be used for diagnostics or medical purposes. The App does not diagnose, treat, mitigate or prevent any disease, symptom, disorder or abnormal physical state. Always seek a health care professional or emergency services if you believe you may have a medical issue.

    Extremely useful!

    This app is extremely helpful for keeping track of most everything that I need to know about what’s happening with my health. It truly helps keep track of my health symptoms throughout the day so I can find patterns and make adjustments to my daily routine in order to better my overall future health. I also love that it communicates with the Apple Health app. It’s very user friendly and easy to use.


    My only complaint is when I manually enter my body temperature on my Apple Watch, it doesn’t actually record it in the app. Maybe it’s a glitch or something…


    It DOES, however, keep track of my wrist temperature whenever I’m wearing my watch, so that’s another helpful feature. It also keeps track of my heart rate and blood oxygen levels (newer models of the Apple Watch have this feature) throughout the day and night, which is VERY important for me to keep track of due to my current health status.


    In summary, it’s an excellent app for tracking symptoms in one place, so you can easily find patterns and share with your doctors and caregivers.
