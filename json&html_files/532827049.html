    Description

    Easily access your Guardian Dental and Vision benefit information.


    - Find a Provider: Search for participating dental or vision providers by name or location

    - ID cards: View, download/add to Apple wallet or email your Dental and Vision ID cards. Use when making an appointment or at your next visit.

    - NEW View Dental benefit plan information

    - NEW View Dental claims status and history

    Initial experience

    I have a new dental plan that began this month with Guardian Direct and set up my account online today. Then I saw the website mentioning how iPhone users can “add your Dental ID card to your Apple wallet…” so I went to my iPhone and searched for Guardian Direct app and I see several variations depending on whether you are a provider, have dental/vision plan, or if you have life insurance plan. I chose this app and I immediately spied after I pressed on “View ID” then the app asks for “User ID” while website asked for my email address. This didn’t work so I pressed on “trouble logging in?” link. I pressed on “Forget User ID” (knowing that isn’t the case), and it asked for my email address. Next screen it says “We are sorry, but your access has been suspended…”. I panicked and went back to website and I still have no problems with website (even logging out and back in). This implies multiple issues with sync’ing between website and app.
