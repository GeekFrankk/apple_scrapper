    Description

    AdventHealth's app offers a range of helpful features.


    See a doctor wherever you need one, no matter the time of day. Receive lab results in your pocket, renew prescriptions from the comfort of your couch, pay your bill conveniently and have your medical records at hand. You can even book an appointment while watching the big game.


    A growing number of Primary Care Patients can chat with a Care Advocate to assist in many areas related to their health journey. Document your progress using the Health Journal or Health Tracking feature. Care Advocacy integrates the AdventHealth App with Apple Health Kit.


    With AdventHealth’s easy-to-use app, getting care on your terms is simpler than ever. Get the cure for the common app.

    My Experience Was Life Saving

    With my prior 2 years experience with heart problems and multiple procedures and test, my aortic valve was discussed multiple times! I was told it wasn’t ready for replacement!

    After a hospital stay in December 2023 I was released from the hospital with shortness of breath and fatigue/weakness and edema in my legs symptoms! My insurance authorized home care nurse and physical therapy. After several weeks my progress was minimal and the shortness of breath and edema had gotten worse! At that point my home care nurse called my nephrologist with concerns of my well being. I was told to go directly to hospital ED and she, the nephrologist , would meet me there! After a couple of test it was determined I needed my aortic valve replaced! I was admitted to Advent Hospital Pepin Heart Institute for the surgery. After a weekend of dialysis I was cleared for surgery and the results were a miracle!

    Now 30 days after TAVR surgery I’m feeling better everyday and currently attending Advent Cardiac Rehabilitation and I’m up to 2 miles on my goal of 3 miles a day in the near future!

    Feeling better than good and thankful to God, my Doctors, Nurses, Technicians at Advent Hospital for saving my life!

    God is Good!🙏

    Developer Response ,

    We are delighted to hear about your remarkable journey to recovery and the outstanding care you received at AdventHealth. Thank you for sharing your inspiring story with us!
