    Description

    Pathway is an accurate and efficient point-of-care reference tool that gives physicians, nurses, physician assistants, pharmacists, residents, and students rapid access to guideline summaries, mobile-friendly interactive algorithms, clinical calculators, landmark trials and more.


    • 40 medical specialties

    • 900+ diseases

    • 75,000+ topics

    • 120+ interactive pathways


    96% 5 Star Reviews

    Trusted by clinicians in over 180 countries


    AS SEEN IN


    • MobiHealthNews: "A well-designed decision support technology adapted to the realities of 21st-century medicine."


    • Physicians Practice: "Provides easier access to important clinical information."


    SIMPLIFY YOUR DAILY PRACTICE


    • Find what you need, faster, with condensed summaries of evidence-based clinical guidelines.


    • Get patient-specific guidance with mobile-friendly interactive algorithms.


    • Easily assess the evidence with color codes, explanations, and links to the original text for seamless due diligence.


    • Quickly reference landmark trial summaries for 500+ diseases, all in one place.


    • Make the right diagnosis by easily consulting pertinent findings when a diagnosis is under question.


    • Find exactly what you need with deep search and an intuitive interface that understands you.


    • Stay up-to-date with notifications for practice-changing updates tailored to your specialty.


    • Get reliable guidance anytime, anywhere - even without an Internet connection.

    EVOLVING EVIDENCE-BASED MEDICINE


    With over 800,000 medical studies published every year, it’s nearly impossible to stay up-to-date on clinical guidelines and best practices.


    Our physician authors and editors painstakingly review, assess, and summarize the highest quality evidence-based clinical information and display it in a clear, simple interface to be acted on at the point of care.


    We master what’s tedious so you can master what’s important—caring for your patients.

    Great idea but could be executed better

    I’m having a bit of hard time reading and comprehending the guidelines on the go because they are a jumbled mix of US/UK/Canadian guidelines and also vary by subspecialty society guidelines. I just want to be able to skim through to the diagnosis / management but it’s not intuitive. I would prefer the option of selecting what regional guidelines I want to follow and then my topics only list those regional guidelines. There are statements with a tiny expand / drop down option to view additional statements. That’s extremely confusing and not fun to use on the phone either. Maybe if user can select region it can shorten the text displayed and the need to have drop down lists.
