    Description

    The fluid and electrolyte calculator helps to calculate the daily maintenance and daily deficit fluids to give dehydrated patients. It also helps to determine how to correct sodium and potassium imbalances.


    In the new update, you can now also calculate the estimated glomerular filtration rate using the standard CKD-EPI Creatinine Equation (2021) and IV fluid drop rate for different micro drip and macro drip IV tubings.
